About Us

This project is a personal machine learning-based system designed to analyze complaints submitted by people against companies. The main objective of this platform is to automatically read complaint text and predict its severity level, helping organizations understand and prioritize customer issues more effectively.

The system uses advanced Natural Language Processing techniques with BERT-based word embeddings combined with a Random Forest classification model to accurately classify complaints into severity categories such as Low, Medium, and High.

This platform is useful for companies, administrators, and users to ensure faster complaint handling, improved customer satisfaction, and data-driven decision making. It demonstrates the practical application of AI and machine learning in solving real-world customer support problems.
